# Company Profile

**Parent Company:** Indices Technology Ltd
**Product:** MarketX
**Country:** Nigeria
**Stage:** MVP — active development

---

## Overview

MarketX is a social commerce platform built for Nigerian markets. It digitises the physical marketplace experience — connecting buyers with nearby sellers through location-based discovery, product reels, and real-time store presence.

Sellers get a fully managed digital storefront. Buyers discover stores, browse live inventory, and transact without needing WhatsApp as a middleman. At the core of the buyer experience is **Dassa**, an AI shopping assistant that handles product discovery, cart management, and order tracking through natural conversation.

---

## Nature of Business

- Social commerce platform (buyer-seller marketplace)
- Location-based store discovery (map, geo-radius search, Market Squares)
- Digital representation of physical Nigerian market zones (Alaba, Computer Village, Bodija, Trade Fair, Ariaria)
- Product media and short-form commerce video (reels)
- Seller SaaS tooling — storefront, inventory, AI listing generation, messaging
- AI-powered shopping assistant (Dassa)
- Payment processing via Paystack

---

## Platform Capabilities

| Layer | What It Does |
|---|---|
| Discovery | GPS map, Market Square zones, radius + filter search |
| Commerce | Product listings with media, deal pricing, Paystack checkout |
| AI — Buyer | Dassa conversational assistant (product search, cart, order tracking) |
| AI — Seller | GPT-4o listing generator (photo → title, description, social captions) |
| AI — Platform | Vector embeddings, semantic search, AI context API for products/sellers/squares |
| Social | Follow stores, shoutouts, activity wall, online presence indicators |
| Seller Tools | Dashboard, business hours, verified badges, premium tiers, messaging inbox |

---

## Target Market

Small and medium traders operating in Nigerian physical markets and commercial districts, and the urban buyers who shop from them.

**Primary cities:** Lagos, with expansion to Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Kano post-validation.
