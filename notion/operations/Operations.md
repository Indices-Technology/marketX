# Operations

> This is how MarketX runs day to day. Every process, decision, and incident is documented here. If it happened but isn't here, it didn't happen officially.

---

## How We Work

| Cadence | What | Who | Where |
|---|---|---|---|
| Daily | Standup update | All active members | Pumble → logged in Notion |
| Weekly | Sprint planning + review | CEO + leads | Notion |
| As needed | Meeting notes | Whoever runs the meeting | Notion |
| As needed | Incident reports | Person who discovers it | Notion |
| Per sprint | Performance notes | CEO | Member profiles |

---

---

## Non-negotiables

1. No task exists unless it is in Notion with an owner, deadline, and deliverable
2. No standup skipped without prior notice on Pumble
3. No decision made in WhatsApp or verbal — Pumble or Notion only
4. Incidents raised the same day they are discovered
5. Sprint review happens every week — no exceptions
