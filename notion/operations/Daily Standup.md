# Daily Standup

> Posted on Pumble every working day by **9:30am**.
> Esther collects and logs the summary here at end of day.
> If you're blocked — say it clearly. Silence is not a status update.

---

## How It Works

1. Each active team member posts their update on the **#standup** Pumble channel by 9:30am
2. Use the template below — no long paragraphs
3. Esther logs the consolidated summary in Notion under the correct week
4. If blocked, tag the CEO directly on Pumble — don't wait for the next standup

---

## Individual Template

```
📅 [Date]
👤 [Your Name]

✅ Yesterday
- [What you completed]

🔨 Today
- [What you're working on]

🚧 Blockers
- [Any blocker, or write "None"]
```
