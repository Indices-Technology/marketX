# Meeting Notes

> Every meeting that involves a decision, direction change, or action point must be documented here before the day ends.
> Verbal agreements not recorded here carry no authority.

---

## How to Log a Meeting

1. Create a new entry below using the template
2. Fill in attendees, decisions, and action points during or immediately after the meeting
3. Tag anyone with an action point on Pumble to confirm they've seen it
4. CEO reviews and countersigns any product or strategic decisions
